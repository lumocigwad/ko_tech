    About Us

    KoTech Developers is an Independent Information and Communication Technology (ICT) Support Company in Kenya specialized in high quality software development, web design and development as well as networking solutions. Our services range from custom development, E-Commerce solutions, Website Design, Mobile Apps Development as well as providing Networking Infrastructure to homes, small and medium sized businesses. This is all achieved through a dynamic team of dedicated, experienced and competent professionals who are committed to delivering the highest level of quality services, products and value to our clients. By constantly focusing on identifying the dynamics of competition and innovations, our solutions place our customers ahead of the curve enabling them to remain relevant in the market. Our strategies, extensive experience and technology enables us to deliver nothing but value and quality. KoTech Developers was founded in the year 2012. Starting out as a small company in Nairobi, the company has since grown with a team of skilled professionals, including software Developers, electrical engineer, product manager and a creative director.The team works tirelessly on the clock to ensure that our clients get more than what they have bargained for. The company has its headquarters in Nairobi and Mombasa Kenya, and plans are underway to expand to other major towns in Kenya as well as the larger East Africa region.
